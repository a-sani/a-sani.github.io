The NEOSSAT Mission

In 2022, I was part of the Satellite Operations team at the Canadian Space Agency as a Software Specialist Intern. During my work term, I had the opportunity to work on two projects for the NEOSSat mission. NEOSSat is the Near-Earth Object Surveillence Satellite for tracking and detecting asteroids, comets, satellites and space debris.

The first project was for processing ephemeris data containing the satellites predicted location at specific times in the near-future. My job was to convert ephemerides from the source format to the Space-Track acceptable format, and to also implement file transfer and retrieval so the ephemeris data could be shared with the public through CSAs new open data server.

The other project focuses on image processing by producing and cleaning raw NEOSSat images files to extract information. My contribution has been to create animations of these images, all in automation.
