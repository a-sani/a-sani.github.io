Wildfire Risk Assessment using Remote Sensing

Dive into the world of predictive analysis and environmental insights with the Wildfire Risk Assessment project. By harnessing multispectral satellite imagery and computing spectral indices, I assessed fire risk variables and conducted post-fire detection. The project's outcome, including maps and graphs, contributes to a deeper understanding of fire behaviors, aiding in forecasting areas prone to forest fires.
