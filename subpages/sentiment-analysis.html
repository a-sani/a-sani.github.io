Sentiment Analysis and Topic Classification GitHub

During a Computational Linguistics course, my team undertook the task of conducting sentiment analysis and topic classifications on scraped news articles, a project that intersects natural language processing and data analysis. I contributed by using opinion lexicon data for sentiment analysis, where I implemented sentiment analysis algorithms for raw-counts on article contents. This involved developing comprehensive Python Notebooks that not only executed the analysis but also provided detailed documentation, highlighting our complex approach.

Additionally, I researched innovative techniques aimed at refining our implementations for future endeavors and for continuous improvement within our team. Collaboratively, we crafted project reports and presentations, distilling our findings and methodologies into the depth of our analysis. Through this effort, we not only demonstrated our understanding of computational linguistics but also honed our ability to translate complex data analyses into accessible insights.
