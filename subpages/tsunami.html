Tsunami Evacuation Site Finder

Coastal British Columbia is very susceptible to major tsunami catastrophes and there does not exist any interactive application to provide the public as a planning tool in case of evacuation during a tsunami event.

The purpose of the Tsunami Evacuation Site Finder application is to help the public identify locations and plan routes to evacuate during an approaching tsunami. The app provides district confirmed evacuation hubs as well as possible municipal sites near coasts that are out of danger zones. The interactive map contains data of historic volcanic eruptions, historic tsunamis, and recent earthquakes to illustrate the regions most susceptible to tsunamis.
